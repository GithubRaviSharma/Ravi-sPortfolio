Cancer Diagnosis Application

Medicos is a medical web application designed to assist in the early detection and diagnosis of breast cancer. It classifies breast cancer cells into benign (non-cancerous) and malignant (cancerous) categories based on various input parameters. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms, Medicos aims to provide accurate and timely diagnoses to support medical professionals in making informed decisions.The web application is user-friendly and designed to streamline the diagnostic process.

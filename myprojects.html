VICCO Web Development Project

Surfing on the internet I stumbled upon the issue that Vikas Cable Company - VICCO being one of the leading cable manufacturers from Delhi had a very old looking website and was lacking the fresh and modern look, I undertook the task of modernizing the online presence of Vikas Cable Company by creating a fresh, dynamic and visually appealing website while retaining all essential information from the previous one. The website is a demo website providing with important informations like products , company qualifications, quality policy & clients.

C# Game Suite: Beginner-Level Games

The C# Game Suite is a curated collection of three beginner-level games developed using C# programming language. 1. Rolling Dice with PC: Test your luck in this simple dice rolling game! Roll the dice against the computer and see who gets the higher number. 2. Rock Paper Scissors with PC: Challenge the computer to a game of Rock Paper Scissors! Choose your weapon and see if you can outsmart the AI opponent. 3. Guess the Number: The computer selects a random number, and your task is to guess it within a limited number of attempts.
